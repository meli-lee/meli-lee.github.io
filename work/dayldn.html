DAYLDN (Dear Asian Youth London) is a youth-led non-profit organisation, a UK chapter of the US-based Dear Asian Youth, that help to educate on ESEA culture and bring together the British ESEA community. I am part of the Web Design team on a voluntary basis and work alongside other volunteers remotely around the UK.
